Τεχνικά Χαρακτηριστικά

*    Max. printing area: 95 x 150 mm.
*    Printing definition: 12 dots/mm (300dpi)
*    Printing speed: up to 50 mm/s
*    Recommended ribbon type: High durability resin belts (RINK method).
